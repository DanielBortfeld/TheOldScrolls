If the game is too difficult, you're stuck or there is no door to go through, go ahead and help yourself:

1. press F10

2. type in one of those:

tgm or togglegodmode -> Toggles God mode
tcl or togglecollision -> Toggles Collision

additem.key -> Adds a Key to your inventory
additem.gold -> Adds a Gold to your inventory
additem.lifecontainer or
additem.life -> Adds a Life Container to your inventory

win -> You win

next or
loadnextmap or
nextmap -> Advance to the next level/map

killallactors or
killallenemies or
clearallenemies or
clearenemies -> Remove enemies from level/map

clearallitems or
clearitems -> Remove items from level/map

clear or
clearmap or
clearall or
clearallentities -> Remove both enemies and item from level/map

3. press enter
